================================================================================

Solution: hsa_amd_memory_lock()
  - Registers CPU-mapped MMIO with GPU MMU
  - Enables safe GPU writes to device doorbells
  - System-scope atomics for PCIe visibility

Architecture:
  ┌────────────┐
  │ CPU Thread │ ──mmap──► cpu_doorbell_ptr (read/debug)
